A sweepstake is a contest where participants have a chance to win a prize. The winner is randomly selected from all eligible participants. The purchase is solely for the eBook, and participation in the sweepstake is an additional opportunity with no purchase necessary to enter.

The sweepstakes are open only to residents of New Mexico, USA, who are 18 years of age or older. To be eligible, you must provide a valid mailing address located in New Mexico. Any participation that does not meet these criteria will be disqualified.

Participation Methods

Participation with Purchase: When you purchase an eBook on our site, you are automatically entered into the corresponding sweepstake. Each sweepstake begins on the launch date indicated on the site and ends on December 31st of each year. The drawing will take place on this date to determine the winners.

Winner Selection
Winners are selected by a random drawing from all eligible participants. The drawing is conducted randomly to ensure fairness. Winners will be notified by email approximately one month after the drawing.

Prize Notification and Claiming
Winners will receive an email at the address provided at the time of participation. They must respond to this email within the specified time to claim their prize. Failure to respond will result in the suspension of the prize, and another winner may be selected.

Prize Value
The prizes offered in the sweepstakes do not exceed a total value of $10,000. Prizes may include televisions, computers, phones, clothing, etc., as indicated on the website.
